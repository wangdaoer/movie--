# SHOT002 Motion Prompt

A worn leather work boot enters the low close-up frame from the upper edge. It gently presses down on a raised wet wooden plank. The plank bends slightly, settles into place, and pushes a thin line of water outward. Small droplets move naturally. The boot does not pause for emphasis; it continues forward and leaves the frame. Steam drifts softly in the background. The shot is quiet, deliberate, and understated.

## Motion Rules

- Foot movement must feel natural and unperformed.
- No face or full body visible.
- Wood motion must be subtle, not exaggerated.
- Water splash must be small and realistic.
- End with the plank flat and still.
