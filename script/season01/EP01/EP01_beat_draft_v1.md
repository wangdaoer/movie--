# WUCHENG / 无昼城

## EP01 Beat Draft V1

Status: Alpha Draft
Episode Title: 灯响前三分钟
Target Length: 45-50 minutes

---

## Core Rule

EP01 is not trying to be perfect. EP01 is the first full production template for the entire WUCHENG pipeline.

---

## Teaser: The Ordinary Day Breaks

### Beat 01: Morning Lamp
A morning lamp turns on before sunrise. Gray Harbor begins with sound before image: wind, steam, rail, radio current.

### Beat 02: The Plank
A raised wooden plank lies on the street. Qi Zhou's worn boot presses it flat without stopping.

### Beat 03: Gray Harbor Wakes
Soup shop, lamp shop, coal cart, workers, steam. The city is alive before the plot begins.

### Beat 04: Qi Zhou Enters
Qi Zhou appears as one worker among many. No heroic entrance. He carries an old dream box.

### Beat 05: The Pause
At the old alley entrance, Qi Zhou slows. The street continues behind him. Only he senses something wrong.

### Beat 06: The Body
In the alley, a worker sits against the wall as if asleep. Qi Zhou observes before touching anything.

### Beat 07: Last Dignity
Qi Zhou adjusts the dead worker's sleeve and badge before beginning work.

### Beat 08: First Collection
The dream box opens. The silver needle touches the ear. A normal white dream crystal forms.

### Beat 09: Blue
The clear crystal shows a faint blue trace. Qi Zhou stops. CUT TO BLACK.

---

## Act One: Hide

### Beat 10: Patrol Procedure
Two patrol workers arrive. They treat the body as routine. Gray Harbor has a system for death.

### Beat 11: Soup Shop Silence
Qi Zhou sits at the soup shop. The soup aunt notices he is late but does not ask why.

### Beat 12: Secret Weight
Qi Zhou unconsciously touches the dream box inside his coat. The audience knows he is carrying something impossible.

### Beat 13: Collection Station
Qi Zhou enters the 13th District dream collection station and places the box on his numbered shelf.

### Beat 14: The Old Man Notices
The old station worker sees the blue trace. His hands tremble for the first time.

### Beat 15: First Violation
The old man locks the box under the counter instead of registering it.

### Beat 16: Warning
The old man tells Qi Zhou: do not tell anyone today.

---

## Act Two: Missing Record

### Beat 17: Normal Work Continues
Qi Zhou tries to continue his day. Every ordinary sound now feels slightly wrong.

### Beat 18: Register Gap
At another office, a missing collection record is flagged. The system notices a hole.

### Beat 19: Wen Xueheng Introduction
Wen Xueheng receives the anomaly report. She does not panic; she starts checking procedure.

### Beat 20: Procedure First
Wen Xueheng asks for timestamps, collector ID, station log, and body transport record.

### Beat 21: Old Man Lies
The old station worker gives a partial answer and hides the box.

### Beat 22: Qi Zhou's First Lie
Qi Zhou is asked whether everything was normal. He says: yes.

---

## Act Three: Investigation Nears Qi Zhou

### Beat 23: Qi Zhou Home
Qi Zhou returns home briefly. Roof leak, iron basin, radio, repaired furniture. His private life is small and worn.

### Beat 24: Blue Still There
He opens the box. The blue trace has not faded.

### Beat 25: Wen Tracks the Path
Wen Xueheng reconstructs Qi Zhou's route: main street, old alley, station.

### Beat 26: Soup Aunt Remembers
Wen asks around indirectly. Soup aunt remembers Qi Zhou was late, but protects him without knowing why.

### Beat 27: Lamp Shop Detail
Old Liang remembers the wind changed near the old alley. This supports Qi Zhou's instinct.

### Beat 28: First Near Miss
Wen passes near Qi Zhou's home but does not knock yet.

---

## Act Four: Pressure

### Beat 29: Station Inspection
An inspector arrives at the collection station asking for the missing item.

### Beat 30: The Old Man Buys Time
The old man claims the item was damaged and needs reprocessing.

### Beat 31: Qi Zhou Decides
Qi Zhou realizes the blue dream cannot simply be handed over. For the first time, he chooses not to follow procedure.

### Beat 32: Wen Finds the Pattern
Wen notices the anomaly resembles a sealed historical case from thirteen years ago, but the file is incomplete.

### Beat 33: Someone Else Knows
A silent observer watches the station from across the street.

---

## Act Five: Hook

### Beat 34: Night in Gray Harbor
Gray Harbor quiets but never sleeps. Wind, water, distant rail, radio.

### Beat 35: Qi Zhou Returns Home
Qi Zhou returns to his leaking room. The iron basin catches water.

### Beat 36: Radio Broadcast
The radio announces tomorrow's work schedule. The voice sounds normal, but the room feels wrong.

### Beat 37: Blue Grows
Qi Zhou opens the dream box. The blue inside the crystal is stronger than before.

### Beat 38: Choice
Qi Zhou does not take the box back to the station. He hides it at home.

### Beat 39: Outside the Window
Under a street lamp outside, a figure stands watching Qi Zhou's window.

### Beat 40: Cut
The iron basin catches one drop of water. CUT TO BLACK.

---

## End State

The audience should know:

1. Qi Zhou is trustworthy.
2. Gray Harbor is real and ordered.
3. Blue dream is impossible.
4. Someone else is already watching.

The audience should not yet know:

1. What blue dream means.
2. What happened thirteen years ago.
3. Who is behind the observation.
4. Where the sun went.
