# WUCHENG / 无昼城

## Pilot Outline V1.0

Status: WIP
Owner: Showrunner
Target: 90-second Pilot Teaser, then EP01 expansion

---

## One-line premise

A tired dream collector stops for a dead stranger on an ordinary morning, and that pause pulls him into a secret that may change the whole city.

---

## Pilot function

The Pilot does not explain the world. It establishes three things:

1. Gray Harbor is alive.
2. Qi Zhou is the kind of person who stops.
3. The blue dream is an exception.

---

## Emotional curve

Quiet -> Familiar -> Uneasy -> Respectful -> Impossible

---

## Sequence 001: Waking

Duration: 0:00-0:30

Purpose: Let the audience enter Gray Harbor before meeting the plot.

Beats:
- A morning lamp turns on in darkness.
- A loose wooden plank is pressed flat by passing boots.
- Gray Harbor begins its ordinary routines: soup, lamps, coal, steam.
- Qi Zhou appears as one worker among many.

Key rule: One shot, one piece of information.

---

## Sequence 002: The Pause

Duration: 0:30-0:55

Purpose: Establish Qi Zhou's instinct before the mystery.

Beats:
- Qi Zhou walks toward the old alley.
- The street continues behind him.
- He stops because something is missing.
- He looks toward the alley.

Audience question: Why did only he stop?

---

## Sequence 003: The Body

Duration: 0:55-1:20

Purpose: Show how Qi Zhou treats death.

Beats:
- A worker sits against the wall as if asleep.
- Qi Zhou observes before touching anything.
- He checks shoes, hands, badge, and posture.
- He adjusts the slipped sleeve.

Key action: Respect before procedure.

---

## Sequence 004: The Collection

Duration: 1:20-1:45

Purpose: Introduce dream collection as a ritual, not a sci-fi device.

Beats:
- The worn dream box opens.
- A single silver needle extends.
- Qi Zhou says quietly: 打扰了。
- The needle approaches the ear.

No music. No glow. No explanation.

---

## Sequence 005: The Exception

Duration: 1:45-1:30 equivalent cut target for teaser

Purpose: End with one impossible image.

Beats:
- A normal dream extraction begins.
- Something blue appears where there should be no blue.
- Qi Zhou stops.
- Cut to black.

Final question: What did he just find?

---

## Character objective

Qi Zhou wants to finish work and get through today.

The story begins because he stops anyway.

---

## Forbidden in Pilot

- No worldbuilding exposition.
- No explanation of dream tax.
- No heroic framing.
- No blue before the blue dream.
- No cyberpunk neon.
